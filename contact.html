Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from clients

How far in advance should I book?

I recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for photography sessions and 4-6 weeks for events or weddings to ensure availability.

Yes, I travel for shoots! Local travel is included in my packages, and I'm available for destination shoots with travel expenses covered.

How long until I receive my photos/videos?

Typically, you'll receive your edited photos within 7-10 days and videos within 14-21 days, depending on the project complexity.

Can I request specific edits?

Absolutely! I welcome your input and can accommodate specific editing requests to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final product.
