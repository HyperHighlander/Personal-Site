About Me

Get to know the artist behind the lens

Creating Visual Stories That Matter

I'm a visual storyteller specializing in photography and videography. With expertise in DaVinci Resolve post-production, I create compelling visual narratives for portraits, events, and cinematic projects.

My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to capture moments that tell powerful stories. Every project is an opportunity to create something unique and memorable.

With over 8 years of experience in the industry, I've developed a unique style that blends technical precision with creative storytelling. My work has been featured in several exhibitions and publications.

My Journey

From passion to profession

Education & Background

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Visual Arts from the San Francisco Art Institute, where I developed my foundation in visual storytelling and technical skills in both photography and videography.

Professional Experience

Over the years, I've worked with various clients ranging from individuals to large corporations. My experience includes:

  • 5+ years of professional photography experience
  • 4+ years of videography and video editing
  • Expertise in DaVinci Resolve color grading
  • Published work in several photography magazines
  • Exhibitions in local art galleries

My Philosophy

I believe that every moment has a story worth telling. My approach is to capture not just images, but emotions and narratives that resonate with viewers. Whether I'm shooting a portrait, covering an event, or creating a cinematic piece, my goal is always to tell a compelling story through visuals.
